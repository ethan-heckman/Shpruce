About The Rustic Spoon

Hi, I'm glad you're here! The Rustic Spoon is a small collection of recipes I actually cook at home — nothing fussy, nothing that requires a trip to three different grocery stores.

Every recipe on this site is tested in a real kitchen, written the way I'd explain it to a friend, and built to work on a normal weeknight.

Have a request or a question about a recipe? Reach out anytime — I'd love to hear from you.
