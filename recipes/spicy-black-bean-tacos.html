Spicy Black Bean Tacos

Smoky, spiced black beans piled into warm tortillas with all your favorite toppings — a 20-minute weeknight win.

🌮
Prep Time10 min
Cook Time10 min
Servings4
DifficultyEasy

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 (15 oz) cans black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • Toppings: avocado, shredded cabbage, salsa, lime wedges, cilantro, cotija cheese

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 3–4 minutes until softened.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds, until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the black beans, cumin, smoked paprika, chili powder, and cayenne. Add a splash of water and mash about half the beans with the back of a spoon for a thicker texture.
  4. Simmer for 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through and slightly thickened. Season with salt to taste.
  5. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or directly over a gas flame for a few seconds per side.
  6. Fill each tortilla with the black bean mixture and top with avocado, cabbage, salsa, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.

Notes

Swap in pinto beans if that's what you have on hand. Leftover filling makes a great burrito bowl or nacho topping the next day.
